# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free community for everyone, regardless of experience level, identity, background, or perspective.

## Expected behavior

- Be constructive, specific, and factual.
- Focus criticism on ideas and work, not people.
- Respect differing technical approaches and lived experiences.
- Assume good intent, ask clarifying questions, and help newcomers participate.
- Respect privacy and do not share another person's private information without permission.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, discriminatory language, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or imagery in project spaces.
- Deliberate disruption, trolling, or sustained bad-faith argument.
- Publishing private, sensitive, or security-related information without authorization.

## Scope and enforcement

This Code applies in project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and community events. Maintainers may remove content, close discussions, reject contributions, or take other appropriate action for behavior that violates it.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to a maintainer through the repository's available contact channel. Do not use public issues for sensitive reports. Security concerns follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
